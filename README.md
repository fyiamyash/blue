# Blue 📧

### Your AI Email Assistant for the Terminal

Blue is a CLI-based AI email agent that helps you manage your inbox using natural language. Instead of manually searching through emails, you can simply ask questions, and Blue will retrieve, read, summarize, and explain your emails directly from your terminal.

## Features

- 📬 **Check your inbox**
  - Ask if you have any new or unread emails.
  - View recent emails without opening your email client.

- 📖 **Read emails**
  - Open and read the contents of any email using natural language.
  - Quickly find important conversations.

- 📝 **Summarize emails**
  - Get concise summaries of long email threads.
  - Understand key points in seconds.

- 📎 **Summarize attachments**
  - Read supported document attachments.
  - Generate AI-powered summaries of PDFs and other supported files without opening them manually.

- 💬 **Natural Language Interface**
  - Interact with your inbox like you're chatting with an assistant.
  - No complicated commands required.

## Example Commands

```bash
blue "Do I have any unread emails?"

blue "Summarize my latest email from Sarah."

blue "Read the email about the design review."

blue "Summarize the PDF attached to the latest email."

blue "What important emails did I receive today?"
```

## Why Blue?

Managing email shouldn't require switching between tabs, searching through threads, or opening multiple attachments.

Blue brings AI directly into your terminal, allowing you to:

- Stay focused while coding.
- Quickly understand important emails.
- Save time by summarizing lengthy conversations.
- Get instant insights from email attachments.
